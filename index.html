Fraud Detection Middleware for UPI Safety

Hackathon Project – Protecting Senior Citizens from Digital Scams

Problem Solution Demo Team

Problem

Senior citizens and digitally less-aware users are often tricked by fraudulent “Payment Request” links. They believe they are receiving money but are actually authorizing a payment by entering their UPI PIN. The lack of contextual warnings in payment apps increases vulnerability to such scams.

Solution

We developed an Android website interface that monitors payment app screens and notification text in real time. It detects high-risk scenarios (e.g., “Request from unknown sender” + UPI PIN entry screen) using rule-based text analysis and triggers a clear, high-visibility warning informing the user: “STOP! You are sending money, not receiving it.”

Demo

Here we showcase how the middleware works:

  • Detects suspicious payment requests.
  • Shows a red warning overlay.
  • Helps users cancel fraudulent transactions.
